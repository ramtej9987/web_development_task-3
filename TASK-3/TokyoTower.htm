Tokyo Tower

Tokyo Tower is a communications and observation tower located in Minato, Tokyo, Japan. Standing at 333 meters tall, it was completed in 1958 as a symbol of Japan's post-war rebirth and modernization. Inspired by the Eiffel Tower in Paris, Tokyo Tower offers panoramic views of the city skyline from its observation decks. It is a prominent landmark and tourist attraction in Tokyo, attracting millions of visitors each year.
