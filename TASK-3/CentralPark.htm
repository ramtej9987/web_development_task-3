Central Park

Central Park is an urban park in Manhattan, New York City, USA. Spanning 843 acres, it is one of the most famous and visited parks in the world. Designed by Frederick Law Olmsted and Calvert Vaux, Central Park offers a green oasis in the heart of the bustling city, featuring lakes, meadows, gardens, playgrounds, and recreational facilities. It is a popular destination for New Yorkers and tourists alike, offering opportunities for relaxation, recreation, and cultural experiences.
